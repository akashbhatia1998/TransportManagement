CHITKARA BUS NOTICE FOR ACADEMIC SESSION 2017-18
1. The Chitkara University has introduced the online Bus facility allotment to facilitate the
students. All the students desirous to avail the bus facility have to apply online through their
CHALKPAD account.
2. The Bus fee for the academic session 2017-18 is as under:-
Destination Charges
(a) Chandigarh, Mohali, Patiala
Kharar & Derabassi 26,000
(b) Panchkula 27,000
(c) Ambala 28,000
(d) Kalka 39,000
(e) Zirakpur & Landran 25,000
(f) Rajpura & Banur 16,000
(g) Sirhand 26,000
(h) Khanna 32,000
(i) Doraha 40,000
(j) Ludhiana 45,000
3. Bus fee will be charged for the complete session 2017-18, except from those who will be
proceeding on Industrial Training. These students are advised to give prior information to
Account department.
4. All the old students who want to avail Transport facility will have to deposit bus fee by 15 June
2017 positively.
5. The students who are facing problem for the online payment are advised to contact Account
Department of the University to deposit the fee manually. Transport office will allot the bus
route and seat to the student manually.
6. No old student will be allowed to board the bus without bus pass/ Bus fee receipt wef 01 July
2017. Any one found travelling without bus pass will be fined Rs 1000/- for the first time. If
checked second time without bus pass then he/she will be considered to be a regular traveler &
charged full bus fee plus a fine of Rs 1000/-.
7. All old students those who have paid bus fee & not made bus pass or travelling without carrying
bus pass will be fined Rs 200/- every time wef 01 Sep 2017.
8. Bus Routes may be revised after the completion of Admission Process for the session 2017-18,
if required.
9. All those students who have been allowed transport facility will have to submit their allotment
letter along with undertaking in Transport Department of University to get the laminated bus
pass issued.
10. The Transportation rules & regulations have been notified on chalkpad.
